Tenmile Creek, Oregon

Since 2022, Sam has conducted several studies, experiments, and summaries on the ecological conditions and characteristics of Tenmile Creek, Oregon. Located south of Yachats, Oregon, Tenmile Creek is composed of various private and public properties. After gaining permission, Sam conducted research in properties managed by Siuslaw National Forest, The Bird Alliance of Oregon (formerly Portland Audubon), and The Worthy Garden Club. While enrolled at Oregon State University, Sam applied the knowledge gained from various courses in practice in Tenmile Creek. Below are unpublished summaries detailing his work.
